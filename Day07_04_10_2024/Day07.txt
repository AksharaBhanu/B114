	+ - * / %

2. assignment operator
	= assign
	+= adds & assign
	-= subtract & assign
	*= multiply & assign
	/= Divide & assign
	%= Modulus & assign

3. increment & decrement operator
	a++  --> post increment
	++a  --> pre increment
	a--  --> post decrement
	--a  --> pre decrement

4. Relational operators (Comparison)
	==	equal to
	!=	not equal to
	>	greater than
	<	less than
	>=	greater than or equal to
	<=	less than or equal to

	i/p: number  o/p: boolean
